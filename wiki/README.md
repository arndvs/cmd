# wiki/

AI-compiled knowledge. The AI maintains these pages from `raw/` sources. You review and correct.

## Subfolders

- `ventures/` — one entity page per venture you own
- `clients/` — one entity page per paid client
- `people/` — one entity page per person in your orbit
- `concepts/` — cross-cutting topics, syntheses, research summaries

## Rules

- **Entity pages are the canonical summary.** Each venture, client, person, or concept gets one page.
- **Cross-reference with `[[wikilinks]]`.** Link related entities so the knowledge graph builds naturally.
- **Update during Ingest and Compile.** When new `raw/` material arrives, the relevant wiki pages get updated.
- **AI writes, operator reviews.** The AI proposes changes. You decide if they're accurate.
