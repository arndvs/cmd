# atlas/

Navigation maps and dashboards. Maps of Content (MOCs) that help you and AI agents navigate the knowledge base.

## What goes here

- **Venture dashboard** — all active ventures by status
- **Client overview** — all engagements by state
- **Content calendar** — what's scheduled, what's in draft
- **Cross-references** — "everything related to X" maps

## Rules

- Atlas pages are navigation aids, not knowledge stores. The knowledge lives in `wiki/`.
- Keep atlas pages updated as ventures and clients change state.
- Use `[[wikilinks]]` to reference wiki pages.
