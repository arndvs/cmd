# Reach Post Prompt

> Goal: wide distribution, draws new audience. Optimized for impressions and new followers.

## When to use

2x per week. These are your top-of-funnel posts that introduce you to people who don't know your work yet.

## Characteristics

- **Hook-first.** The insight is in the first line, not buried at the end.
- **Relatable.** Hits a pain or pattern the audience recognizes immediately.
- **Shareable.** Someone saves it or sends it to a coworker.
- **Short.** 50-150 words. Punch, don't pad.

## Prompt template

```
Write a reach post for [platform].

Topic: [one-line topic from ideas/vault.md]

Pillar: [engineering / AI products / agent engineering]

Voice rules:
- Read content/voice/style-guide.md and content/voice/samples.md first
- Match the tone, rhythm, and vocabulary from the samples
- Check against content/voice/anti-samples.md before finalizing

Structure:
1. Hook — one sentence that stops the scroll
2. The observation — 2-3 sentences unpacking the insight
3. The twist or reframe — why it matters more than they think
4. Close — one line that lands

Constraints:
- No corporate speak
- No "I'm excited to share"
- Specific > generic (name tools, numbers, outcomes)
- Under 150 words
```

## Examples of good reach topics

- Counterintuitive take on a common practice
- "The thing nobody tells you about [X]"
- Quick pattern you've seen across multiple projects
- Deleting code / removing complexity stories
